About Us

At Nami, omakase is an expression of trust. Each evening unfolds as a quiet dialogue between chef, ingredient, and guest—guided by seasonality, precision, and restraint.

Our menu is shaped daily by what is at its peak, sourced from trusted fisheries and markets, both local and Japanese. Nothing is rushed. Every cut, every movement, every course is prepared with intention, allowing the natural character of each ingredient to speak for itself.

While our menu offers a curated structure, the spirit of omakase remains at the core of the experience. Guests are guided through thoughtfully composed courses and selections, each reflecting the season and the chef’s perspective. Whether choosing individual offerings or a tasting progression, every dish is prepared with the same attention to balance, technique, and timing.

Nami takes its name from the Japanese word for “wave,” a reflection of the rhythm that guides our craft: constant, deliberate, and evolving. No two evenings are the same, yet each follows the same commitment—to refinement without excess, and to an experience that lingers long after the final course.
